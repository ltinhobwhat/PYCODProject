Password Security Tips

  • Use at least 8 characters with a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  • Avoid common words, names, or personal information
  • Consider using passphrases: "Coffee!Sunrise#42" is stronger than "P@ssw0rd"
  • Each website should have a unique password
  • In real life, use a password manager to generate and store secure passwords
